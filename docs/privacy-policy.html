Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026  |  App: Aura SMS (com.novachat)

Aura is a privacy-first SMS & MMS messaging app. Your messages, contacts, and personal data stay on your device. We do not operate servers that collect, store, or process your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Aura stores the following data exclusively on your device. None of this data is transmitted to us or to any server we operate.

Data Type Purpose
SMS & MMS messages Read from and written to the Android system SMS provider so you can send, receive, and view messages
Contact names & photos Displayed alongside conversations for identification; cached in memory only
Spam/block rules Your custom block rules (number, keyword, language) stored in a local database
Spam learning data On-device machine learning model trained from your spam/not-spam feedback
App preferences Theme selection, notification settings, Do Not Disturb schedule, swipe actions, and other settings
Scheduled messages Messages you schedule for later delivery
Conversation metadata Pin, mute, archive status, and per-conversation notification preferences

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your messages on any external server.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not use crash reporting services.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any account information.
  • We do not create user profiles or share data with third parties for advertising.

2. Android Permissions

Aura requests the following permissions, each of which is necessary for core functionality:

Permission Why It's Needed
READ_SMS, RECEIVE_SMS, SEND_SMS, WRITE_SMS Required to function as your default SMS app — to read, send, and receive text messages
RECEIVE_MMS, RECEIVE_WAP_PUSH Required to receive MMS multimedia messages as a default SMS app
READ_CONTACTS To display contact names and photos in conversations, and to identify known contacts for spam filtering
READ_PHONE_STATE To format phone numbers correctly and support WhatsApp forwarding
INTERNET Used solely for Google Play Billing (in-app purchases) and to download the on-device ML Kit language model
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To show notifications for incoming messages
VIBRATE To provide haptic feedback for notifications
FOREGROUND_SERVICE To run scheduled tasks like auto-backup and scheduled message delivery

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google Play Billing

Aura uses Google Play Billing for the optional lifetime premium purchase. When you make a purchase, your transaction is processed entirely by Google. We receive only the purchase state and product identifier — we do not receive or store your payment details. Google's privacy policy applies to this data: policies.google.com/privacy.

3.2 Google ML Kit (On-Device Language Identification)

Aura uses Google ML Kit for on-device language detection to support language-based block rules. Your message text is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to Google servers. However, ML Kit may collect non-content metadata (such as device model, app package name, and performance metrics) in accordance with Google's ML Kit terms: developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms.

3.3 WhatsApp Forwarding

Aura offers an optional feature to forward messages to WhatsApp. This is done by opening WhatsApp via a deep link with a pre-filled recipient and message. Aura does not send your data to any intermediary server. Once in WhatsApp, that app's own privacy policy governs: whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Spam & Scam Detection

Aura's Smart & Secure spam and scam detection is 100% on-device. It uses rule-based pattern matching, heuristic analysis, and a local learning model trained from your feedback. No message content is ever uploaded to external servers for analysis. All spam scores, keyword weights, and sender reputation data remain in your device's local database.

5. Backup & Restore

Aura allows you to create backups of your app settings and spam learning data. Backups are saved to a location you choose (such as your device storage, Downloads folder, or a cloud drive). Aura does not automatically upload backups to any cloud service. If you choose to save a backup to Google Drive or another cloud service, that service's privacy policy applies.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Messages: Stored in the Android system SMS provider. Deleting a conversation in Aura removes it from your device's SMS database.
  • Spam data: Stored locally. Uninstalling Aura removes all spam learning data, block rules, and app settings from your device.
  • Preferences: Stored locally in Android DataStore. Removed when you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Since we do not collect or store any data on our servers, there is no data to request deletion of from us.

7. Children's Privacy

Aura is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted by the app, there is no children's data at risk.

8. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device and protected by Android's built-in app sandboxing. Each app's data is isolated and inaccessible to other apps. We recommend securing your device with a screen lock to protect your messages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
